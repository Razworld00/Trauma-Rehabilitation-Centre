Trauma & Rehabilitation Centre is more than a school.

We are a registered NPO (257-320) dedicated to academic excellence and social healing in the Eastern Cape. From matric rewrites to Level 5 Social Auxiliary Work, we equip individuals with the skills and empathy to rebuild lives and communities.

Our holistic approach reflects our unwavering commitment to fostering academic excellence, personal growth, and societal impact, exactly as outlined in our official profile.
